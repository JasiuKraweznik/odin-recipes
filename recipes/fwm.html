Fries with mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 500g mushrooms
  • 20g olive oil
  • 300g fries
  • salt
  • spices like pepper, marjoram, oregano, sweet pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220Â°C
  2. in the meantime peel mushrooms
  3. Lay parchment paper on baking tray and fold this paper in midpoint to not let mushroom water get to fries
  4. place fries and mushrooms on different halfs
  5. pour over mushrooms with olive oil
  6. salt fries and mushrooms
  7. season fries with spices at your discretion
  8. put tray to oven
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until fries will be goldren brown, about 18 minutes
  10. serve hot
