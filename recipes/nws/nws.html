Noodles with spinach

Description

Vegan and glutenfree noodles with spinach and tomato

serving for two

Ingredients

  • 250g Corn noodles
  • 15g olive oil
  • 2 big onions
  • 4 big garlic cloves
  • 1 big tomato
  • 500g frozen shredded spinach
  • salt
  • spices like pepper,thyme, marjoram, oregano

Steps

  1. boil water in a pot
  2. salt water to get flavor
  3. when the water starts to boil, put the noodles in it, about 1-12 minutes
  4. in the meantime warm up olive oil on pan
  5. dice the onions, tomato and cut the garlic into slices
  6. put onions onto the pan and fry over medium heat until vitrify
  7. add spinach to onion and fry it until onion will be goldren brown and spinach hot
  8. add garlic and tomato and season spinach with spices at your discretion
  9. after 2-3 minutes turn off the gas and drain the noodles
  10. put everything on a plate and mix
  11. serve hot
