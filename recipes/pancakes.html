Vegan Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 130g oat flakes
  • 2 medium bananas
  • 70g potato flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 tablespoon olive oil
  • fruits like strawberry, blueberry. banana, pineapple

Steps

  1. Put oat flakes,2 bananas, potato flour, water and ground cinnamon in the blender and mix them
  2. warm up olive oil on pan
  3. Pour batter onto pan
  4. Brown on both sides
  5. Add fruits
  6. serve hot
